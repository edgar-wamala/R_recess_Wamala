About


Bukooli College is a government aided senior secondary school which was founded by the community in 1981. The school is located in Ndifakulya “A” parish, Western Division of Bugiri Municipality, Bugiri District in Eastern Uganda. Bugiri district is multi- ethnic with various tribes settled in the different sub counties. So Bukooli College Bugiri is the biggest secondary school in the District where majority of parents in the district take their children there specifically for learning purposes and instill knowledge in them.
